My top 3 movies of all time.

Drive

This movie influenced and rolemodeled some of my masculine ideals.
There is a certain masculine stoicism embodied by the main character,
who is nameless throughout the movie, and a desire to protect others.
There is also an appreciation for driving as a hobby and as an aesthetic.
I have watched this movie at least half a dozen times by now.

Iron Giant

I first watched this movie as a young boy, and have since watched it several times.
This movie shows the meaning of sacrafice for those we love,
the importance of loyalty and brotherhood,
and that anyone is capable of changing themselves and their destiny,
regardless of who they were born as.

They Live

This movie breaks the fourth wall of American culture.
It shows us what we are surrounded by but never see.
This movie is thought provoking and highlights the fact that we all can be and are influnced
in ways that may not be apparanent to us or anyone we know.
